  • A Population Forecasting Analysis

    "A Population Forecasting Analysis" is a project created by our team of four girls for the "Naan Mudhalvan" course. Using Tableau, a data visualization tool, we analyzed population trends for a specific geographic area and made predictions about future growth or decline. To begin the project, we gathered data on different factors. We then used Tableau to create visualizations such as line charts, bar graphs, and maps to showcase our findings and highlight trends and patterns in the data.

    Overall, the project aimed to provide insight into the population trends of the area and help inform policy decisions related to healthcare, education, and infrastructure. By analyzing and forecasting population growth or decline, we provided valuable information for stakeholders to consider when planning for the future of the area.
